1736 <p>

1737My clients obtain consistently positive results for their brain health and savvy mental health <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/diet" title="Psychology Today looks at nutrition" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1738nutrition</a>

1739 when they add hemp into their diet. I suggest to them that they eat hemp daily in the form of organic hemp oil, raw hemp seeds and hemp milk. It is an ideal way to provide support for brain focus, balanced mood and a pain-free body. Culinary hemp oil may be added to salad dressings or smoothies (just a tablespoon a day) and I provide you with two recipes below.</p>

1744<p>

1745Hemp is a rich source of plant- based protein, and <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/omega-3" title="Psychology Today looks at Omega 3" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1746Omega 3</a>

1747 and 6 fatty acids. Hemp is also an excellent good source of GLA (gamma linoleic acid) and it is an ideal to decrease the inflammation of <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/depression" title="Psychology Today looks at depression" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1748depression</a>

1749, autoimmune disorders and <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/hormones" title="Psychology Today looks at hormonal" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1750hormonal</a>

1751 imbalances, that can occur with peri <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/menopause" title="Psychology Today looks at menopause" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1752menopause</a>

1753, menopause and andropause. Auto immune and hormonal imbalances occur at a significant rate in people with <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/post-traumatic-stress-disorder" title="Psychology Today looks at PTSD" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1754PTSD</a>

1755, histories of chronic <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/stress" title="Psychology Today looks at stress" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1756stress</a>

1758Rhythms of Recovery: Trauma Nature and the Body </a>

1759about the relationships between PTSD and physical illness. GLA from Hemp is especially useful for helping children and adults who experience the co-occurring complex of dermatological and mental health imbalances of psoriasis, eczema and acne along with depression and <a href="https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/anxiety" title="Psychology Today looks at anxiety" class="basics-link" hreflang="en">

1760anxiety</a>

1761.</p>
